How it works

From campaign idea
to live collab.

Post a campaign, get matched with the right partner or creator, and let Lumni's AI write the brief. You focus on the relationship — we handle the creative.

01

Set up your campaign

Choose platform, content format, integration type. Define what you're offering and what you need in return — service, product, or cash-equivalent credits.

02

Match with a partner

Lumni matches you with a compatible brand or creator based on audience, category, and campaign fit. You approve, they accept.

03

Get your AI brief

Lumni generates 3 ready-to-shoot creative scenarios — hooks, scene structure, CTA, tone. Pick one, customize, or generate more.
